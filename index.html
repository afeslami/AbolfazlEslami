    About

    Professor Abolfazl Eslami received PhD degree from the University of Ottawa under the supervision of Prof. B.H. Fellenius in 1996. His research fellowship followed up at the University of British Columbia (UBC) under the mentorship of the late Prof. R.G. Campanella, in 1994. He completed his B.Sc. degree in Civil Engineering at Sharif University of Technology (SUT) in 1985 and received an M.Sc. degree in Geotechnical Engineering at Tehran Polytechnic (AUT), Tehran, Iran in 1988. He has served as a professor at the Universities of Guilan and Amirkabir University of Technology (AUT) from 1997 up to the present. Prof. Eslami is an internationally recognized authority in the field of in-situ testing and foundation engineering, and, in particular, in deep foundations. He has gained a wealth of practical experience during 40 years of work at home and overseas as founder and chairman of Sham-e Consulting Engineering Company through a variety of assignments that encompass foundation systems, ground improvement methods and stability issues for residential buildings, industrial plants, bridges, and marine structures. He has written up over 110 peer-reviewed Journal papers and 80 conference papers. Prof. Eslami has gained six national invention patents and published seven books. The latest one is "Piezocone and Cone Penetration Test (CPTu & CPT) Applications in Foundation Engineering", Elsevier Publisher, 2020.
