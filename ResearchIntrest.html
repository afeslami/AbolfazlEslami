    Major Accomplishments

    • Developing Unicone method (Eslami & Fellenius, CGJ, 1997), implemented worldwide for pile design using CPT and CPTu records
    • Fabrication of Frustum Confining Vessel (FCV-AUT), Physical modeling apparatus for CPT, helical and anchor piles testing, simulating field situations up to 250 kPa
    • Compiling AUT; Geo-CPT & Pile Database comprising 600 records of pile axial loading, tests along with adjacent CPT or CPTu profiles including offshore and nearshore records
    • Publication novel papers on foundation engineering issues in well-known international journals such as OE, SoilDyn, RMGE, Geotechnique, COMGEO, DFI, CGJ, ICE, MGG, Georisk, ASCE (IJOG), PEM, TRIG and SAOS
    • Upgrading 1g Apparatus for Adjacent Foundations Study Along with Image Processing
    • Serving as senior advisor and supervisor for the feasibility study of a novel gravity base foundation system for offshore wind turbines in the North Sea, in 2022
    • Giving various postgraduate courses such as Marine Geotechnical Engineering, Application of Deep Foundations in Offshore Structures, Ground Improvement, Advanced Foundation Engineering, Site Investigation, In-situ Tests Applications in Geotechnical Engineering
    • Collaborating as a peer referee for several well-known journals, such as: OE, SoilDyn, RMGE, Geotechnique, COMGEO, and CGJ
    • Organizing and holding several workshops on CPT & CPTu applications, foundation systems, adjacent buildings construction, ground improvement, uncertainty & reliability applications, and sustainable geo-structures
    • Citation Based on Google Scholar Over 3780 cases and with H-Index 33
    • Invited Paper for Special Issue in Probabilistic Engineering Mechanics Journal by Prof. Phoon and Dr. Tang: Heidarie Golafzani, S. and Eslami, A. (2023), CPT and Pile database design approach; a site-specific method upon reliability and statistical assessment criteria.
    • Invited Paper for Fellenius Issue: Eslami, A., Moshfeghi, S., Heidari, S., Valikhah, F. (2019). AUT: Geo-CPT&Pile Database Updates and Implementations for Pile Geotechnical Design. Geotechnical Engineering Journal of the SEAGS & AGSSEA, Volume 50 Issues 2 2019-5
    • Development and Implementation of Frustum Confining Vessel (FCV) for Penetration and Pile Testing, FCV-AUT, 2014.
    • Five Inventions Licensed by Iranian Researches and Scientific Organization, 2013-2014
    • Hot Paper, in Elsevier 2009 Journal of Computers and Geotechnics By:Ardalan, H. Eslami, A. Nariman-Zadeh, N. " Piles shaft capacity from CPT and CPTu data by polynomial neural networks and genetic algorithms".
    • Software Development, UniCone, a program for Processing and Reporting of Cone Penetration Tests (CPT and CPTu), Soil Profiling and pile Capacity Analysis. Unisoft Ltd., 1905 Alexander Street, Calgary, Alberta, T2G 4J3.
