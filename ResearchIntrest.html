    Major Accomplishments

      - Developing Unicone method (Eslami & Fellenius, DFI (Fulcrum), 1995; CGJ, 1997), implemented worldwide for pile design using CPT and CPTu records

      - Fabrication of Frustum Confining Vessel (FCV-AUT), Large-scale physical modeling apparatus for CPT, helical and anchor piles testing, simulating field situations up to 250 kPa

      - Compiling AUT; Geo-CPT & Pile Database comprising 600 records of pile axial loading

      - tests along with adjacent CPT or CPTu profiles including offshore and nearshore records

      - Publication novel papers on deep foundations issues in well-known international journals such as DFI, CGJ, ICE, MGG, Georisk, ASCE (IJOG), PEM and SAOS

      - Serving as senior advisor and supervisor for the feasibility study of a novel gravity base foundation system for offshore wind turbines in the North Sea, in 2022

      - Giving various postgraduate courses such as Marine Geotechnical Engineering, Application of Deep Foundations in Offshore Structures, Ground Improvement, Advanced Foundation Engineering, Site Investigation, In-situ Tests Applications in Geotechnical Engineering

      - Collaborating as a peer referee for several well-known journals

      - Organizing and holding several workshops on CPT & CPTu applications, foundation systems, adjacent buildings construction, ground improvement, uncertainty & reliability applications, and sustainable geo-structures

      - Being involved as co-author of Advanced Foundation Engineering book under an agreement with Wiley Publisher Co. and visiting scholar in UCSD in 2023
